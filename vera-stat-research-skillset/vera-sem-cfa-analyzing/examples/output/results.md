## Results

The three-factor CFA converged successfully in N = 301. Overall fit was adequate, chi-square(24) = 85.31, p < .001, CFI = 0.931, TLI = 0.896, RMSEA = 0.092 (90% CI [0.071, 0.114]), and SRMR = 0.065. All standardized loadings were positive and statistically significant (all p < .001), ranging from 0.424 to 0.855. The weakest indicator was x2 (loading = 0.424), whereas the strongest was x5 (loading = 0.855). Interfactor correlations ranged from 0.283 to 0.471; the largest was between visual and speed (r = 0.471, p < .001), indicating moderate but not extreme construct overlap.
