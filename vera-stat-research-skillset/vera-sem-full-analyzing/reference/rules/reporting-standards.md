# Full SEM Reporting Standards

1. Report sample size, estimator, global fit indices, and key structural coefficients together
2. Distinguish measurement results from structural path results
3. For mediation, report direct, indirect, and total effects with inferential detail; use bootstrap CI when possible
4. Endogenous latent-variable `R²` should be reported when available
5. When both direct and indirect paths are significant, describe the pattern as consistent with partial mediation rather than proof of causality
6. Do not call SEM paths "causal" unless the design warrants it
7. Alternative models should be theory-driven, not post hoc fishing
