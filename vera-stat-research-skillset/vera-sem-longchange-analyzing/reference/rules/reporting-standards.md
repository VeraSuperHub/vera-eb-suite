# Longitudinal SEM Reporting Standards

1. Report sample size, estimator, and the time coding explicitly
2. State whether change is modeled as growth, latent basis, or change score
3. Report latent intercept and slope means, variances, and their covariance with inferential detail
4. If measurement invariance is assumed across waves, say so explicitly
5. Distinguish average change from between-person heterogeneity in change
6. Avoid causal language unless the design supports it
7. Nonlinear improvements must be justified by theory and fit, not fit only
