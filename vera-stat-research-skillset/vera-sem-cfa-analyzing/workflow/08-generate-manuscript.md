# Step 08 — Generate CFA Manuscript Files

Produce:

- `methods.md` with software, estimator, identification approach, fit criteria, and validity procedures
- `results.md` with fit indices, loadings, reliability, validity, and invariance findings
- `tables/` for loadings, fit, reliability, and invariance comparisons
- `figures/` for path/loading diagram and any invariance or loading visual
- `references.bib`

Required narrative components:

1. Why CFA was used
2. How indicators were assigned to constructs
3. Which estimator and why
4. Whether reliability / validity support the constructs
5. Whether invariance supports group comparisons
