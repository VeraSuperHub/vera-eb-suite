# Step 08 — Generate Full SEM Manuscript Files

Produce:

- `methods.md` with measurement model, structural model, estimator, fit criteria, and bootstrap procedure
- `results.md` with fit, loadings, key paths, indirects, and subgroup comparisons
- `tables/` for fit indices, paths, indirect effects, and model comparison
- `figures/` for SEM diagrams
- `references.bib`

The final narrative should explain both whether the overall SEM fit and what the
structural relations imply substantively.
