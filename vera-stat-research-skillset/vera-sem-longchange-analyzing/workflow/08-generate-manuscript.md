# Step 08 — Generate Longitudinal SEM Manuscript Files

Produce:

- `methods.md` with wave structure, time coding, model type, software, and fit criteria
- `results.md` with fit, latent means/variances, and subgroup trajectory findings
- `tables/` for fit comparison and latent parameters
- `figures/` for trajectory diagrams/plots
- `references.bib`

The results narrative should explain what changed, how fast, and for whom.
