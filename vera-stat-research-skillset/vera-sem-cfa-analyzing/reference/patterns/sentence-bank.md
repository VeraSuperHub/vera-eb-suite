# CFA Sentence Bank

## Fit

- "The hypothesized measurement model showed acceptable global fit, χ²(df) = [val], CFI = [val], TLI = [val], RMSEA = [val], and SRMR = [val]."
- "Global fit was borderline, with CFI/TLI near conventional targets and RMSEA/SRMR indicating [interpretation]."

## Loadings

- "Standardized loadings were all salient (range: [low] to [high]), supporting the intended factor structure."
- "Most indicators loaded strongly on their intended factor, although [indicator] was comparatively weak."

## Reliability / Validity

- "Composite reliability and omega supported internal consistency for all latent constructs."
- "Average variance extracted was adequate for [constructs], while [construct] remained below the usual convergence target."

## Invariance

- "Configural, metric, and scalar models were compared sequentially; changes in CFI and RMSEA supported [level] invariance."
- "Scalar invariance was not fully supported, suggesting that latent mean comparisons should be interpreted cautiously."
