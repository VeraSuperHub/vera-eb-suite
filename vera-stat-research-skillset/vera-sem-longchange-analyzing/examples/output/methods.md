## Methods

A linear latent growth curve model was fit in R using lavaan on 400 observations from the Demo.growth dataset. A latent intercept factor loaded equally on four repeated measures, and a latent slope factor used fixed linear time scores of 0, 1, 2, and 3. The model was estimated with ML and an explicit mean structure. Model fit was evaluated with chi-square, CFI, TLI, RMSEA with 90% confidence interval, and SRMR. Interpretation focused on latent intercept and slope means, their variances, and the intercept-slope covariance.
