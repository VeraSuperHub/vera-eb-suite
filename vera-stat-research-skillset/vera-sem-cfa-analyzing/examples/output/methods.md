## Methods

A confirmatory factor analysis was fit in R using lavaan on 301 observations from the HolzingerSwineford1939 dataset. A three-factor measurement model was specified for visual, textual, and speed ability, with latent variances standardized (`std.lv = TRUE`) and estimation by ML. Model fit was evaluated with chi-square, CFI, TLI, RMSEA with 90% confidence interval, and SRMR. Standardized loadings and interfactor correlations were reviewed to characterize measurement strength and construct overlap.
