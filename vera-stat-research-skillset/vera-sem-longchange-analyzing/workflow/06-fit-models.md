# Step 06 — Fit Alternative Longitudinal SEMs

Fit only the models that answer a real developmental question:

1. Linear latent growth
2. Latent-basis or freed-loading growth
3. Quadratic growth if enough waves exist
4. Latent change score
5. Parallel-process growth when two coordinated trajectories are available

Prefer a small, theory-driven model set over exhaustive fishing.
